Guideline 1:Neonates Have an Immature Immune System

The body has a unique system of defense designed to protect against infections and foreign substances.Exposure to an infectious agent activates a complex series of responses to identify,neutralize,and eliminate the invading organism.


Neonates have an immature immune system and an impaired ability to effectively eliminate invading organisms.


Preterm infants are at even greater diadvantages than term infants.The younger the gestation,the higher the risk for infection and an advance outcome.
